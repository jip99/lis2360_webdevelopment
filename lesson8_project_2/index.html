Importance

Without a sense of organization to keep things easily accessbile, things are bound to be lost and inaccessible, resulting in potential troubles and chaos. The concept of databases follows this same philosophy, where they are crucial in keeping everything they handle, from credit card accounts to confidential government documents, in an organized, concise, and practical system to prevent these bad situations from occurring.

Definition

According to Techopedia, they define databases as being organized collections of data that allow data to be easily accessible, manipulated, and updated. Essentially, you can think of databases being like a very big virtual filing cabinet.

Some incentive to work with databases. It'll blow your mind.

According to Glassdoor, the average salary of a database administrator is around eighty thousand dollars, with the higher offers easily making six-figures. With this in mind, businesses pay these consistent salaries to database workers since database management and maintenance is a highly desired skillset for growing businesses wishing to optimize their use of databases.

What type of pay can you expect to see? See for yourself.

A database analyst can earn an average of an average $67,000 a year, and database managers can make more at around $74,000 a year. However, database administrators can make anywhere between $80,000 and $100,000 a year on average, depending on how vital the position is.
